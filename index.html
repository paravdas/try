About Restech

Driving the global digital transformation of the real estate industry through innovative, secure, and scalable software solutions.

Welcome to Real Estate Software Technology Private Limited (Restech), a pioneering force in the global PropTech industry. Headquartered in the bustling tech hub of Bengaluru, India, with a growing international footprint, we specialize in delivering enterprise-grade digital infrastructure tailored exclusively for the real estate sector. Our mission is simple yet profound: to empower real estate agencies, brokers, developers, and property managers with next-generation software solutions that drive growth, streamline operations, and enhance customer experiences.

In an era where digital transformation is no longer optional but essential, Restech stands out as a trusted technology partner. We understand that the real estate market is highly competitive and rapidly evolving. Therefore, we do not just build websites; we architect comprehensive digital ecosystems. Our core expertise encompasses dynamic property portals with advanced IDX/MLS integrations, highly customized Customer Relationship Management (CRM) systems, and native mobile applications for both iOS and Android platforms.

What sets us apart is our deep industry knowledge combined with our technical prowess. Our team of over 50 in-house expert developers leverages cutting-edge technologies—including React, Node.js, Python, AWS, and Flutter—to create solutions that are secure, scalable, and lightning-fast. We recognize that trust and security are paramount when dealing with high-value property transactions and sensitive client data. That is why we strictly adhere to global compliance standards, implementing robust security protocols, end-to-end encryption, and secure third-party integrations like Docusign and Stripe.

At Restech, we believe in a transparent, agile, and client-centric development process. From the initial discovery call to wireframing, sprint-based development, and final deployment, we maintain open lines of communication, ensuring that our clients are involved at every step. We operate seamlessly across multiple time zones, providing 24/7 support and strategic IT consulting to our partners in the USA, UK, UAE, Australia, and beyond.

Our track record speaks for itself, with over 3,000 projects delivered successfully and millions saved in development costs for our clients. By choosing Real Estate Software Technology Private Limited, you are not merely hiring a coding agency; you are partnering with a dedicated team of innovators committed to building your digital real estate empire. Let us help you turn your technological vision into a profitable reality, ensuring you stay ahead of the curve in the global real estate market.
