About Restech

Driving the global digital transformation of the real estate industry through innovative, secure, and scalable PropTech solutions.

Welcome to Real Estate Software Technology Private Limited (Restech), a pioneering force in the global PropTech industry. Headquartered in Bengaluru, India, we specialize in delivering enterprise-grade digital infrastructure tailored exclusively for real estate businesses globally.
